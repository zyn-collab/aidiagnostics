## Summary

| Body Region       | Categories | Findings |
|-------------------|-----------|----------|
| Abdomen-Pelvis CT | 17        | ~218*    |
| Chest CT          | 13        | 93       |
| Head CT           | 8         | 29       |
| Breast MRI        | 9         | 40*      |
| **Total**         | **47**    | **~380** |

*Note: The YAML config files contain slightly more questions than the paper's stated counts of 210 (abdomen) and 35 (breast). The paper states 366 total evaluated tasks. Some questions in the YAML may be near-duplicates or alternate phrasings (e.g., "bowel obstruction" appears twice in the GI category, "hiatal hernia" appears twice). The official evaluation used 366 distinct binary classification tasks: 210 abdomen-pelvis + 92 chest + 29 head + 35 breast.

## Performance Summary
- Overall mean AUC: 0.87 across 350+ findings (UCSF test set)
- Abdomen-Pelvis CT: 86.4 mean AUROC
- Chest CT: 88.0 mean AUROC
- Head CT: 90.1 mean AUROC
- Breast MRI: 82.9 mean AUROC
- Outperforms Google MedGemma, Microsoft MI2, Alibaba Lingshu, and Stanford Merlin
- Best in 87.2% (319/366) of tasks
